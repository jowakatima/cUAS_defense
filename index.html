SAM BATTERY [1]

Surface-to-air interceptor battery. Long range, kinetic kill — destroys any target in one hit, including cruise missiles.

EW JAMMER [2]

Electronic attack against C2 & GPS links. Jams every susceptible target in range at once — but only threats with a known waveform. Targets marked ◇ (autonomous / fiber-optic) sail right through.

$150 emplace · free to operate

HEL — HIGH ENERGY LASER [3]

Directed energy. Burns through any target type at near-zero cost per shot — but engages one target at a time and needs dwell time to burn through. Tougher airframes take longer.

$600 emplace · free per shot

HPM — HIGH POWER MICROWAVE [4]

Wide-area RF pulse that fries electronics of everything in the beam — the swarm killer. Works on any target without needing a waveform library, but hardened airframes shrug off most of each pulse.

$550 emplace · free per pulse
